Operations manager, supply chain

Position Overview:

We are looking for an experienced Operations Manager to lead our Supply Chain operations and ensure the smooth flow of materials, information, and products through our supply chain network. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in supply chain management, with expertise in inventory management, logistics, and process optimization. This role will be responsible for coordinating with internal teams, external vendors, and logistics partners to drive operational excellence and meet customer demand.

Responsibilities:

Oversee all aspects of the Supply Chain operations, including procurement, inventory management, warehousing, and distribution. Develop and implement strategic plans and initiatives to optimize the efficiency and effectiveness of the supply chain network. Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including procurement, manufacturing, sales, and finance, to align supply chain activities with business objectives. Manage inventory levels to ensure adequate stock levels while minimizing excess inventory and carrying costs. Negotiate contracts and agreements with suppliers, vendors, and logistics partners to secure favorable terms and pricing. Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics to track operational performance and identify areas for improvement. Implement process improvements and best practices to streamline operations and reduce costs. Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards related to supply chain operations. Lead and develop a team of supply chain professionals, providing coaching, mentorship, and guidance as needed.

Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Business Administration, or a related field. Master's degree preferred. Proven experience in supply chain management, with a minimum of [X] years of experience in a similar role. Strong understanding of supply chain principles and practices, including inventory management, logistics, procurement, and distribution. Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to analyze complex data sets and draw actionable insights. Proficiency in supply chain software and systems, such as ERP systems (e.g., SAP, Oracle) and inventory management tools. Demonstrated leadership and management skills, with the ability to motivate and inspire teams to achieve results. Effective communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate with internal and external stakeholders. Detail-oriented with a focus on accuracy and precision in all aspects of work. Ability to thrive in a fast-paced and dynamic environment, managing multiple priorities and deadlines simultaneously.

Preferred Qualifications:

Professional certifications in supply chain management or related fields (e.g., APICS, CSCMP). Experience with Lean Six Sigma or other continuous improvement methodologies. Knowledge of international trade and customs regulations. Familiarity with e-commerce and omnichannel distribution strategies. Previous experience in the [specific industry or sector relevant to your company].

Benefits:

Competitive salary Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance Retirement savings plan with company matching Flexible work schedule and remote work options Professional development opportunities Casual work environment with a collaborative culture
